About inTox

Nowadays, every government seems to be interested in what we're saying online. inTox is built on a "privacy goes first" agenda, and we make no compromises. Your safety is our top priority, and there isn't anything in the world that will change that.

Security

inTox takes your privacy seriously.
With leading-class encryption, you can rest assured knowing that the only people reading your messages are the ones you send them to.

Ease of Use

Unlike other secure messaging solutions, inTox does not require you to be a computer programmer to use it. inTox comes out-of-the-box with an easy-to-use interface that allows you to focus on your conversations.

Freedom

inTox is both free for you to use, and free for you to change. You are completely free to both use and modify inTox. Furthermore, inTox will never harass you with ads, or require you to pay for features.
